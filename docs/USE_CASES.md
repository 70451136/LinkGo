# 使用场景 / Use Cases

## 中文

### 1. 译员与本地化团队

LinkGo 适合处理跨格式、多轮次、需要术语一致性的翻译任务。

常见任务：

- Word 合同、报告、说明书翻译。
- PowerPoint 演示稿翻译并保持版式。
- Excel 表格、清单、产品资料翻译。
- 字幕和视频内容翻译。
- 译后校对和问题表格输出。

价值：

- 减少复制粘贴。
- 降低格式损坏风险。
- 复用术语库和翻译记忆。
- 把校对问题整理成可行动清单。

### 2. 企业知识与文档团队

企业文档往往涉及固定术语、品牌表达、审批流程和合规要求。LinkGo 可以把术语库、翻译记忆和长期偏好变成可复用资产。

常见任务：

- 内部制度、培训材料、产品手册多语言处理。
- 客户交付文档翻译和审校。
- 长期项目术语沉淀。
- 多文件批量整理和摘要。

### 3. 内容创作者与运营团队

LinkGo 可用于从文档、字幕、视频和灵感材料中快速生成可交付内容。

常见任务：

- 视频字幕翻译。
- 跨语言宣传文案整理。
- PPT 内容生成和排版辅助。
- 灵感库内容沉淀。
- 多平台内容本地化。

### 4. 技术、制造与工程文档

技术资料通常包含大量缩写、单位、编号、图表和专业术语。LinkGo 的校对流程会特别关注数字、单位和术语一致性。

常见任务：

- 技术规格书翻译。
- 工程说明和设备手册翻译。
- CAD / DXF 相关文本处理。
- 术语和缩写复核。

### 5. 本机自动化与高级用户

通过 LinkClaw，用户可以让本机智能体在沙箱中处理目录和文件。

常见任务：

- 批量整理本机工作区。
- 生成脚本、小工具或报告。
- 调用 LinkGo 文件管道处理复杂格式。
- 让智能体连续完成多步骤任务。

## English

### 1. Translators and Localization Teams

LinkGo is useful for cross-format translation tasks that require layout preservation, terminology consistency, and review workflows.

Common tasks:

- Translating Word contracts, reports, and manuals.
- Translating PowerPoint decks while keeping layouts.
- Translating Excel sheets, lists, and product data.
- Translating subtitles and video content.
- Reviewing translations and exporting issue tables.

Value:

- Less copy-paste work.
- Lower risk of format damage.
- Reusable glossaries and translation memories.
- Actionable review findings.

### 2. Enterprise Knowledge and Documentation Teams

Enterprise documentation often depends on controlled terminology, brand voice, approval workflows, and compliance. LinkGo helps turn terminology, translation memory, and long-term preferences into reusable assets.

Common tasks:

- Multilingual internal policies, training materials, and product manuals.
- Customer-facing document translation and review.
- Long-term terminology accumulation.
- Multi-file summarization and organization.

### 3. Content Creators and Operations Teams

LinkGo can help turn documents, subtitles, videos, and inspiration materials into deliverable content.

Common tasks:

- Video subtitle translation.
- Cross-language marketing copy.
- PowerPoint content generation and layout support.
- Inspiration library content creation.
- Multi-platform content localization.

### 4. Technical, Manufacturing, and Engineering Documents

Technical materials often include abbreviations, units, identifiers, diagrams, and domain terminology. LinkGo's review workflow pays special attention to numbers, units, and terminology consistency.

Common tasks:

- Technical specification translation.
- Engineering instructions and equipment manuals.
- CAD / DXF text-related workflows.
- Term and abbreviation review.

### 5. Local Automation and Advanced Users

With LinkClaw, users can let local agents process directories and files inside a sandbox.

Common tasks:

- Batch-organizing local workspaces.
- Generating scripts, small tools, or reports.
- Calling LinkGo file pipelines for complex formats.
- Letting agents complete multi-step tasks.
